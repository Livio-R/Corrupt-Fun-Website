The First Tip/Burn/Gamble Bot created on Shibarium.



ABOUT

The Corrupt Fun Bot is a Telegram-based tool that enables projects to tip, burn, and gamble with onboarded tokens.

TIP

Reward your community members who actively contribute through engagement, memes, and/or artwork.

BURN

The bot allows for token burning to decrease supply, with a leaderboard to monitor the top contributers.

GAMBLE

Users can engage in games like blackjack and 13, utilizing any losses to support project development.

Visit t.me/corruptfun to integrate the Corrupt Fun Bot in your project's Telegram.
